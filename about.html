About our
Company

Started by a single mother of three boys with a passion for creating healthy snacks. Los Chicos, the boys in English. We at Los Chicos produce healthy snacks for all ages.

VISION

In a world filled with heavily processed and unhealthy snacks, it's increasingly difficult to find
nutritious options that are enjoyable and filled with nutrients. With a growing number of
health-conscious consumers around the world, the demand for vegetable-based snacks is higher than ever.










MISSION

Creating a healthier world, one snack at a time.
